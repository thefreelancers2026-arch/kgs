Our Story

From a modest workshop in Tamil Nadu to a regional authority in style.

Founded in 2012 in the heart of Virudhachalam, KGS Home Decors began with a singular vision: to prove that world-class elegance didn't have to be out of reach. What started as a boutique selection of local artisanal pieces has blossomed into THE CURATED ATELIER—a destination for those who seek more from their interiors.

For over a decade, we have navigated the intersection of tradition and innovation. Our journey is paved with partnerships with local craftsmen and international designers, ensuring every texture, material, and form tells a story of heritage and hope.

We don't just sell furniture; we provide the canvas for your life's most meaningful moments. Welcome to a decade of dedication. Welcome to the Atelier.
