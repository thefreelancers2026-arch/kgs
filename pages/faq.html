shopping_cart Ordering

How to place an order expand_more
Simply browse our collections, select your desired pieces, and add them to your bag. You can proceed to checkout by clicking the bag icon at the top right. For a more personalized experience, you can also place orders via our WhatsApp concierge.
Payment methods expand_more
We accept a wide range of secure payment options including UPI (GPay, PhonePe), Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking processed through Razorpay. Cash on Delivery (COD) is also available for select regions.
WhatsApp ordering expand_more
Prefer chatting? Click the WhatsApp icon on the product page to connect with our curator who can process your order manually and assist with any styling questions.

local_shipping Shipping & Delivery

Delivery across Tamil Nadu expand_more
We specialize in delivery across all major districts of Tamil Nadu. Whether you're in Chennai, Coimbatore, or Madurai, our premium white-glove service ensures your decor arrives in pristine condition.
Delivery timeline & Free shipping expand_more
Orders are typically delivered within 3-7 business days. We are pleased to offer complimentary standard delivery for all orders exceeding ₹2000.

assignment_return Returns & Exchanges

Return policy & Damaged goods expand_more
We maintain a rigorous quality check process. However, if your item arrives damaged, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. We will arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.
Refund process expand_more
Once an eligible return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.

inventory_2 Products

Crafting timeless pieces for modern homes.

Product authenticity & care expand_more
Every piece from KGS Home Decors is verified for authenticity and craftsmanship. We provide specific care instructions with each item—ranging from soft dusting for our sculptures to specialized cleaning for our textiles.
Custom orders expand_more
For select collections, we offer customization in size and finish. Please contact our Atelier team to discuss your specific requirements for bespoke interior solutions.

account_circle Account

Creating an account & Password reset expand_more
Click the profile icon in the navigation bar to create your account. This allows you to track orders and save your wishlist. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to receive a reset link via email.
Order tracking expand_more
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also view live status updates within the "My Orders" section of your account profile.
